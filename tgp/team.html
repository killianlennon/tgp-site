Our Team

The people behind
the place.

The passionate crew of golf nuts and coaches that make up The Golf Place.

Founder & General Manager
Killian Lennon

Killian grew up in Wrightstown, PA and built his golf obsession on local Bucks County courses. A true data junkie, his motivation for The Golf Place was to make improvement more tangible & bring golf enthusiasts together. When not at The Golf Place, you can find him campaigning for the 10 foot gimme circle to be implemented outdoors.

General Manager
Justin Hepler

A lifelong golfer hooked on all things professional golf and all of the cutting edge technology that comes along with it. Motivated to bring a hospitable "country-club esque" environment to indoor golf in the Bucks County area. From a military background and a Temple alum, outside of TGP Justin loves tennis and all things professional sports.

Assistant General Manager
Gavin Riley

Gavin played club golf at Kutztown University after starting out at Neshaminy High School. He's been around the game for years — not just playing, but working in it as a certified club fitter, which gave him a real appreciation for the details behind every swing. Outside of golf, he's probably talking about football.

Director of Golf · PGA Pro
Logan Buell

Logan grew up in Yardley where he graduated from Pennsbury, High School. Logan has held Assistant Golf Professional roles at both Cherry Valley & Trenton Country Club. When not on the golf course Logan enjoys spending time with friends and family & going to the beach.

Founder of Imagen Golf
Daniel Guest
Top 100 CoachPXG Staff ProTPI CertifiedTrackMan Certified

Daniel is the Founder of Imagen Golf and a Top 100 coach with over 39,000 lessons taught across seven countries. He brings a science-based, biomechanics-first approach to every session — no generic tips, no one-size-fits-all methods.
