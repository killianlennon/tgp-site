Teaching Philosophy

Your swing.
Your blueprint.

Daniel doesn't teach a model. He teaches you. Every student has a unique physical makeup — and the most efficient swing is the one built around your body, not someone else's.

Using biomechanics and his proprietary 7-7-7 Protocol, Daniel diagnoses your swing's most damaging flaws and fixes them fast — without tearing everything down and starting over.
