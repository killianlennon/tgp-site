    Your Coach

    Logan Buell
    Director of Golf

    PGA Associate Director of Golf 7 Years Teaching Junior Golf Specialist

    Logan Buell is a PGA Associate and the Director of Golf at Fairways & Dreams. With seven years of professional teaching experience — most recently as an Assistant Pro at Trenton Country Club — Logan brings serious on-course credentials to every lesson.

    During his time at TCC, Logan was instrumental in revitalizing the youth golf scene, leading the Junior Golf program for three years and successfully doubling the size of the PGA Junior League team.

    Teaching Philosophy

    Built around
    your game.

    Logan's instructional philosophy is simple: there's no one-size-fits-all approach to golf. He focuses on personalized swing analysis that works with each player's natural body mechanics.

    His versatile background ranges from fine-tuning the games of Division I collegiate athletes to introducing beginners to their very first swing.
