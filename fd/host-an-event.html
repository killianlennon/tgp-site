    What We Offer

    Golf, drinks, and great food — all in one place.

    Fairways & Dreams combines a premium golf simulator facility with a full bar and private event space. Six TrackMan bays, PuttView putting green, PJ Whelihan's catering, and an atmosphere that makes every event feel like a real night out.

    Private Venue Hire

    Book the whole facility for your group — simulators, bar, putting green, and event space. Total privacy for the duration of your event.

    Full Bar & Party Area

    One of the things that sets Fairways & Dreams apart — a full bar setup and dedicated party area right alongside the simulators. Food, drinks, and golf in one space.

    PJ Whelihan's Catering

    Order from PJ Whelihan's directly to your event — wings, sliders, shareable platters, the full menu. Delivered straight to your bays so the game never stops.

    PuttView Putting Green

    Fairways & Dreams now has PuttView — augmented reality putting that adds a whole new dimension to your event. Putting contests and games available for all skill levels.

    Corporate Outings

    Team-building, client entertainment, or company celebrations — the bar, simulators, and putting green create a complete experience your guests won't forget.

    Custom Packages

    Every event is different. We'll work with you on bay count, duration, food, drinks, and any extras to build a package that fits your group perfectly.

    The Venue

    6 bays. A full bar. One great night.

    Fairways & Dreams isn't just a simulator facility — it's a proper venue. Six TrackMan bays, a full bar and lounge area, a PuttView putting green, and a staff that makes every event run smoothly.

    Every bay runs TrackMan 4, giving your guests access to the same technology used by the world's best players. Over 500 virtual courses available — play Augusta, St Andrews, Pebble Beach, and more.

    Corporate Events

    Team-building, client hospitality, company parties. The combination of golf, bar, and private space gives corporate events a feel that a conference room never could.

    Birthday Parties

    Simulators, a full bar, great food, and your whole crew. Book the night and let the venue do the work — we've got everything you need for an unforgettable birthday.

    Pre-game before the main event, or make Fairways & Dreams the whole night. Either way — great golf, great drinks, and the best group activity in the area.
