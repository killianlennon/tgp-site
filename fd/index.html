About Fairways & Dreams

The place where golfers get better.

Join a growing community of Montgomery County golfers who are serious about improving their game. Fairways & Dreams gives you the tools, technology, and instruction to see exactly where you stand — and how to get better.

Part of the TGP family: Fairways & Dreams is now proudly part of The Golf Place network. Members enjoy immediate reciprocal access to our Langhorne location — one membership, two premium facilities.
