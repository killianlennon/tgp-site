Our Team

The people behind
the place.

The passionate crew of golf nuts and coaches that make up Fairways & Dreams.

Founder & General Manager
Killian Lennon

Killian grew up in Wrightstown, PA and built his golf obsession on local Bucks County courses. A true data junkie, his motivation for The Golf Place was to make improvement more tangible & bring golf enthusiasts together. When not at The Golf Place, you can find him campaigning for the 10 foot gimme circle to be implemented outdoors.

Director of Golf · Fairways & Dreams
Logan Buell
PGA ProHead Coach

Logan grew up in Yardley where he graduated from Pennsbury, High School. Logan has held Assistant Golf Professional roles at both Cherry Valley & Trenton Country Club. When not on the golf course Logan enjoys spending time with friends and family & going to the beach.

Assistant General Manager
Glenn MacPherson

After a long career in entertainment retail, working with bears and sharks and playing golf in his spare time, it was only fitting that Glenn joined Fairways & Dreams in 2022. Born and bred in Philly, Glenn attended Villanova University and brings his passion for a great guest experience to all visitors. When possible, he still finds time for golfing, following all the local sports teams, especially the Birds, and spending time with his family and dogs down the shore.
