Cancellation Policy

We ask for 24 hours notice to cancel or modify your reservation so we can offer your bay to other golfers.

  • Cancellations made more than 24 hours in advance receive a full refund.
  • Cancellations within 24 hours of your reservation will be charged the full hourly rate for the time booked.
  • Late arrivals: we hold your bay for 15 minutes. After that, we reserve the right to re-book it.

Good to Know

  • All bays are priced per hour, per bay — bring as many people as you like.
  • Members log in through the MindBody app for member pricing.
  • Gift cards are accepted at checkout.
  • Questions? Contact us and we'll help.
⛳
Booking with Kids & Teens Under 18

We welcome golfers of all ages. Parents/guardians are responsible for their group's conduct. Max 4 people per bay (including non-players), only 1 player swinging at a time — swinging a club with others in the bay is extremely dangerous. Minors under 16 must be accompanied by an adult. Management reserves the right to end any session if safety rules aren't followed.
